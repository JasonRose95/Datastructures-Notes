##########################What-Are-Data-Structures?#################################

-simple concept needed for coding interview

-coding = manipulating data to accomplish something
*i.e. requests, transforming, displaying, storing, retrieving, returning

-data structures = organizing & managing data
*i.e. emails are a datastructure organized in vertical list w/ folders
can be managed in order recieved

^code organizes and manages data in emails

-data structure = collection of data values, relationships among them, and
functions/relations that can be applied to them

-we can look for, insert, remove, and add numbers to datastructures

-different data structures have different relations and functions

-be well versed in popular data structures to know relationships, functions,
benefits/downsides that all data structures have
